Vegan Caramel Chocolate Cups recipe

SERVES: 6

PREPARATION TIME: 10 mins

DIFFICULTY: Easy

INGREDIENTS

  • 7 tbsp oat flour
  • 2 tbsp almond flour
  • 3 dates
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • Vegan chocolate
  • 1 tsp coconat oil
  • 1 tbsp date syrup

METHOD

Soak dates in the hot water about 10 mins. Then, mash it.

Mix oat flour, almond flour, almond butter, date syrup and coconat oil well. You can add 1 or 2 tbsp warm water if needed.

Split into 6 pices. And fill into the muffin cups and leave the middle with gaps.

fill the cups with mashed dates. Chill in fridge about 5 mins

Melt the chocolate via Bainn Maria method

Pour onto cups, chill in fridge about 2 hours

ENJOY IT
